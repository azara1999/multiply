Standard Method

Example: 66 X 13

Steps

  1. Multiply 6 x 3 to get 18. Leave the 8 and carry the 1.
  2. Multiply 3 x 6 to get 18 but don't forget to add the 1 to ge 19.
  3. Put a place holder under the 8.
