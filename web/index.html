Multiplying Two Digit Numbers with Two Digit Numbers!



Standard Method

Example: 66 X 13

Steps

  1. Multiply 6 x 3 to get 18. Leave the 8 and carry the 1.
  2. Multiply 3 x 6 to get 18 but don't forget to add the 1 to get 19.
  3. Put a place holder under the 8.
  4. Multiply 1 x 6 to get 6. Put the 6 under the 9.
  5. Multiply 1 x 6 again to get 6. Put the 6 under the 1.
  6. Now add up the 2 columns of numbers, 198 and 66.
  7. After you add the columns you should have 858 for your answer!.

Partial Product Method

Example: 66 X 13

Steps

  1. Multiply 6 x 10 to get 60..
  2. Multiply 6 x 3 to get 18..
  3. Multiply 60 x 10 to get 600..
  4. Multiply 60 x 3 to get 180..
  5. Add all partial products up: 600 + 180 + 60 + 18.
  6. After you add the numbers up, your final answer should be 858!.
